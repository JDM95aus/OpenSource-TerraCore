## The Vision

A future where every home and community can produce its own nutritious, diverse food. Not through traditional farming, but with a quiet, solar-powered appliance that requires no land, minimal water, and creates zero waste.

This project is a detailed blueprint for a self-sustaining system that automates the cultivation of oyster mushrooms and transforms this biomass into a wide range of familiar foods through mechanical texturization and precise nutrient fortification.
